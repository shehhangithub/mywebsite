A weekday in my life

5:15am-5:50am

Waking up

I wake up and get ready for school by eating breakfast, brushing my teeth and changing into my school uniform.

5:50am-2:00pm

School

I reach school by 6:30am and school starts at 7:40am. I have many lessons during school for example on Friday, where I have LA (english), Math, PE, Science and History. As a class chairperson, I have to carry out the activities during CCE lessons planned by the Student Council

3:00pm-7:00pm

CCA

I have to reach Macritchie Reservior by 3:00pm everyday that I have CCA and we will paddle from 3:50pm-6:15pm. I use a C1 Nelo which is a canoe boat which means that you have to paddle on 1 side only.

8:00pm-10:00pm

At home

I reach home at 8:00pm and eat dinner, shower, then do homework, read books or play games before sleeping.
